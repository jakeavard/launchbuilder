About

Jake Avard is a dynamic Catholic speaker who brings passion, clarity, and conviction to every conversation. Wheter addressing faith, culture, or leadership, Jake connects with audiences through a blend of real-world experience, professional style, and personal vulnerability.

"Jake has a clear calling and a gift for connecting with audiences and I highly reccomend him for any speaking engagements where a strong, heartfel, and mission-driven voice is needed." Ashley Mann | Account Executive | Pierpont Communications.

Jake's passion: "I've always been drive to find and share truth - especially through meaningful conversation. My goal isn't just to inform, but to invite people into deeper thought, prayer, and discipleship.

Why choose Jake as a speaker?

Jake Avard brings expertise across a wide range of areas including evangelization, apologetics, Catholic social teaching, faith and finances, family life and pro-life advocacy, conflict resolution, and public discourse. He equips audiences to share their story, defend the faith with reason and charity, and navigate cultural and moral challenges with clarity and love.

Why choose Jake as a speaker? He tailors every talk to the unique needs of your audience—whether a parish mission, college event, high school retreat, conference, or leadership team. With a background in corporate finance (B.B.A. in Finance + 3 years of experience) and years of ministry practice, he blends practical expertise with Gospel values, offering insights that are both compelling and actionable.

Jake is available for both in-person and virtual events, bringing a dynamic blend of leadership, care, and persuasive clarity. His goal is not just to inform, but to inspire, invite reflection, and encourage discipleship, leaving audiences motivated to engage their faith and their world with joy, conviction, and love.
