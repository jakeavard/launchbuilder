About

Jake Avard is a dynamic Catholic speaker who brings passion, clarity, and conviction to every conversation. Wheter addressing faith, culture, or leadership, Jake connects with audiences through a blend of real-world experience, professional style, and personal vulnerability.

"Jake has a clear calling and a gift for connecting with audiences and I highly reccomend him for any speaking engagements where a strong, heartfel, and mission-driven voice is needed." Ashley Mann | Account Executive | Pierpont Communications.

Jake's passion: "I've always been drive to find and share truth - especially through meaningful conversation. My goal isn't just to inform, but to invite people into deeper thought, prayer, and discipleship.

Why choose Jake as a speaker?

Jake Avard equips audiences to share their faith with clarity and conviction. From evangelization and apologetics to Catholic social teaching, family life, and faith-filled finances, he offers practical tools to navigate life’s challenges with reason, love, and Gospel-centered insight.

Every talk is tailored to your audience—whether a parish mission, college campus, high school retreat, conference, or leadership team. Drawing on a B.B.A. in Finance, three years in corporate leadership, and years of ministry experience, Jake blends professional expertise with pastoral care, making complex topics relatable and actionable.

Available for in-person and virtual events, Jake inspires audiences to think, pray, and engage with the world with joy, courage, and conviction. His talks don’t just inform—they invite deeper reflection, discipleship, and a stronger commitment to living faith boldly in every area of life.
